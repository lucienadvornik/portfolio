Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2025

This website uses cookies to enhance your experience and to help us understand how you use our site. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which types we use, and how you can control them.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site recognize your device and remember information about your visit.

2. Types of cookies we use

Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the operation of the website. They cannot be turned off in our systems and are usually only set in response to actions made by you such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in, or filling in forms.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously (e.g., via Google Analytics).

  • Tool: Google Analytics
  • Collected data: device info, anonymized IP, page visits, time spent
  • Retention: up to 24 months
  • Provider: Google Ireland Ltd.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to show you personalized ads and remarketing content based on your browsing activity.

  • Tool: Google Ads
  • Purpose: Remarketing, conversion tracking, user segmentation
  • Collected data: device ID, browser behavior, ad interactions
  • Provider: Google Ireland Ltd.

3. Managing your preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences using the consent banner shown when you first visit the site. You can also reopen the banner anytime to change or withdraw your consent.

Additionally, you can manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that blocking some types of cookies may impact your experience on the website.
